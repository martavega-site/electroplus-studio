Studio Notes

Studio A in London features a live room, configurable layout, outboard gear, NS10 and Focal monitors, and Otari 1/4 inch tape. Studio B in Stockholm is a writing, production, and mix suite with a Neumann U87, vintage guitars and amps, UAD, Neve, DBX, Juno 106, and vintage synths.

Studio A / Squarehead

London

Beautiful sounding live room with a highly configurable layout, a control room with plenty of outboard gear, and a setup designed for recording bands, solo artists, and voice sessions.

  • AML Neve microphone preamplifiers
  • API, Focusrite, Distressor, Avalon, 1176, Neve, SSL, DBX
  • NS10 and Focal monitors
  • Otari 1/4 inch and large drums & keys collection
Studio B / Electroplus HQ

Stockholm

Writing, production, and mix suite designed as the ideal place to start or finish a record, built around a compact but character-rich setup for modern production work.

  • Neumann U87
  • Vintage guitars & amps
  • UAD, Neve, DBX
  • Juno 106 and vintage synths
